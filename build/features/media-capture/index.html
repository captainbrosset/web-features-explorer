Media capture

The navigator.mediaDevices.getUserMedia() API requests access to devices that produce audio or video streams, such as microphones or video cameras.

Compatibility

  • Chrome 53
  • Chrome on Android 53
  • Edge 12
  • Firefox 39
  • Firefox on Android 39
  • Safari 11
  • Safari on iOS 11
